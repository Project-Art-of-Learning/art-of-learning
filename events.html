College Panel

The annual Art of Learning College Panel hosts Project Art of Learning Alumni entering, currently enrolled in, and graduating from top colleges (MIT, Yale, UPenn, UChicago, etc.) as they talk about their experiences!

What to expect:

  • Insights from alumni at prestigious universities
  • College application tips and advice
  • Q&A sessions with current students
  • Networking opportunities
  • Information about different academic programs
