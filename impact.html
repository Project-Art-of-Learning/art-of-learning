Our Global Reach

From our headquarters in San Diego to students around the world, Project Art of Learning has established a network that transcends borders. We serve students from diverse backgrounds, including refugees, immigrants, and underserved communities, providing them with the educational support they need to succeed.

Our established chapters and client regions span across North America, Europe, and Asia, demonstrating our commitment to making quality education accessible to all students, regardless of their geographic location or economic circumstances.
