Partners and Sponsors

The organizations that help make our work possible.

Our Partners

The International Rescue Committee has supported victims of humanitarian crises in 40+ countries since 1933. For more information, visit www.rescue.org.

Hope for San Diego engages volunteers across San Diego to help those in need. For more information, visit hopeforsd.org.

Joey's Wings funds kidney cancer research and spreads awareness for cancer patients since 2014 in memory of Joey Xu. For more information, visit joeywings.org.

The University of Puerto Rico, Arecibo is a public college in Arecibo, Puerto Rico founded in 1967. For more information, visit upra.edu.

Education in Action empowers students in Texas through educational opportunities. For more information, visit educationinaction.org.

Advancing Students Forward provides education to underserved students. For more information, visit advancingstudentsforward.org.

SD Refugee Tutoring provides education for refugees in San Diego. For more information, visit https://sdrefugeetutoring.com.

Best Kids provides tutoring for kids in foster care. For more information, visit https://www.bestkids.org.

Our Sponsors

Leonardo DRS develops defense technologies, namely in naval and maritime systems. For more information, visit leonardodrs.com.
